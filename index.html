A B O U T

Hi! I am Juno! A GRAPHIC & WEB DESIGNER based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Skilled in several design software and with a strong background in graphic design. During my two years of experience at the agency, I have designed corporate websites, e-commerce websites, university websites and app, a total of 17 projects.

With a background in information design, I have a greater sensitivity to the ordering of information on websites and the format in which it is presented. I am committed to integrating brand design with web themes in order to enhance web recognition under the premise of meeting the client's needs.
